Rice Mill Management System Description

The Rice Mill Management System is a comprehensive business management application developed to digitize and simplify the daily operations of a rice mill. It provides a centralized platform to manage paddy processing orders, employee records, wages, sales, stock inventory, profit and loss tracking, and other operational expenses.

The system offers real-time visibility into paddy processing stages, available stock levels, employee wage payments, and overall financial performance. By automating manual workflows and maintaining accurate records, it helps rice mill owners and administrators improve efficiency, reduce errors, and make informed operational and financial decisions.
